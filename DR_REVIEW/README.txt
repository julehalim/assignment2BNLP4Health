+++ ./ratemd-dr-ratings.csv +++
This file consists of patient review data derived from Wallace et al. A large-scale quantitative analysis of latent factors and sentiment in online doctor reviews.  JAMIA 2014 (https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/24918109/) (see ./PDF directory).  The file consists of 54,017 comments scraped from the www.ratemds.com website -- a US service to review physicians.  Each review is associated with a patient rating (-1 or 1).
